Features

What it
actually does.

Calorie trackers tell you what you ate. DINR.ai tells you what to eat. That's the difference.

Full macro breakdown

Protein, calories, fat, carbs for every dish on the menu. Takes a couple of seconds.

Your goals, your rules

Bulking? Cutting? Just trying to not blow your calorie budget on a Tuesday? Set it and go.

Ranked, not listed

Dishes are scored and sorted. The best match for you is at the top. No scrolling through 40 items.

Before, not after

MyFitnessPal makes you photograph your food after you've eaten it. We tell you what to pick before you order.

Thousands of menus

We pull menu data from restaurant databases across the UK. If they have a menu, we can probably find it.

Your data stays yours

We don't sell your info. No shady tracking. UK GDPR compliant. Read our privacy policy.
